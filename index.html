How to Play

Ultimate Secret Number is a group game for 2-10 players.

  1. A secret number between 1 and 99 is chosen.
  2. Players take turns trying to guess the number.
  3. To make a guess, you must first correctly answer a trivia question.
  4. If you answer incorrectly, your turn is skipped in the current round.
  5. When guessing, the range narrows based on your guess.
  6. If you guess too high, that becomes the new upper bound.
  7. If you guess too low, that becomes the new lower bound.
  8. The player who is forced to guess the secret number loses!

Custom Question Bank
